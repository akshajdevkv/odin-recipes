Classic Italian Panzanella Salad

This refreshing Italian panzanella salad combines fresh summer tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion, and crusty bread, tossed in a tangy vinaigrette. Perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish at a barbecue!

  • 4 cups cubed day-old rustic bread
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. For the vinaigrette, whisk together red wine vinegar, olive oil, Dijon mustard, sugar, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
  2. Place cubed bread in a large mixing bowl. Drizzle vinaigrette over bread, tossing to coat thoroughly.
  3. Add the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and fresh basil. Toss to combine, ensuring the bread soaks up the dressing and the vegetables are evenly distributed.
  4. Let the salad sit for at least 15 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. Can be refrigerated for up to 1 day.
  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy!
